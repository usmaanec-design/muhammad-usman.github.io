Featured Project: Smart Waste Management System (MWMSR)
I am actively working on the Smart Waste Management System (MWMSR) project for Zehran Company, focused on optimizing municipal waste collection and operational efficiency. My role involves daily scheduling of vehicles, assigning duties to labour teams and foremen, and ensuring smooth task distribution to area supervisors based on real-time needs.

I utilize ArcGIS mapping to design and optimize waste collection routes, minimizing fuel consumption and time. Additionally, I manage daily operational planning, covering area-wise cleaning tasks, resource allocation, and progress tracking across multiple districts.

The system also includes a real-time monitoring dashboard for supervisors and management, enabling effective decision-making and quick response to on-ground challenges. I oversee the coordination between vehicles, labour teams, and supervisors, ensuring all activities are aligned with project goals and client expectations.

My responsibilities extend to data management, performance reporting, and continuous process improvement, aiming to deliver a smarter, more sustainable waste management solution.
